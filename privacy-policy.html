Privacy Policy for SharedNote

Last updated: 2025.09.04

Introduction

SharedNote (“we,” “our,” or “the App”) respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your data when you use our application.

By using SharedNote, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

Information We Collect

  • No personal data collection by the App: SharedNote does not require registration or login. We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or any other personal information directly.
  • No server storage: All your notes are stored locally on your device. We do not transfer or store your data on external servers.

Advertising

SharedNote displays ads provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect certain information automatically, including but not limited to:

  • Device information (such as device type, OS version, identifiers for advertising)
  • IP address, approximate location (for regional ad targeting)
  • Interaction data with advertisements

Google and its partners may use cookies, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies to serve ads based on your interests. You can learn more about how Google uses your data here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/ads

You can opt out of personalized ads through your device settings (Google Settings → Ads → Opt out of Ads Personalization).

How We Use Your Information

Since we do not collect personal information directly, the only data processing may occur through advertising partners (Google AdMob) to display relevant ads. We do not use your data for any other purposes.

Data Sharing

The only way data leaves your device is:

  • If you actively use the Share function to copy a note to another app (e.g., Telegram, email, messenger) or transfer it to another device with SharedNote installed.
  • Through advertising services (Google AdMob), which may process device-related and ad-interaction information.

The App does not automatically send your notes or personal content to any servers.

Third-Party Services

SharedNote uses the following third-party service:

  • Google AdMob – for displaying advertisements.

Please review Google’s Privacy Policy here: https://policies.google.com/privacy

Data Security

All notes are saved locally on your device. We recommend using device-level security (PIN, fingerprint, password) to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Children’s Privacy

SharedNote is safe for all ages. However, since the app displays ads, children should use the app under parental supervision. We do not knowingly collect or process any personal information from children.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be reflected with a new “Last updated” date.
